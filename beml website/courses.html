Our Programs

Pre-University (PU) Courses

Our Pre-University programs are designed to provide a strong foundation for higher education, offering a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical application across various streams.

Science

Focuses on fundamental scientific principles and prepares students for engineering, medical, and research fields.

  • PCMB (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology)
  • PCMC (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Computer Science)

Commerce

Provides a comprehensive understanding of business, finance, and economics, ideal for careers in commerce and management.

  • CEBA (Computer Science, Economics, Business Studies, Accountancy)
  • HEBA (History, Economics, Business Studies, Accountancy)

Arts

Explores humanities and social sciences, fostering critical thinking and analytical skills for diverse career paths.

  • HESP (History, Economics, Sociology, Political Science)
  • HEPS (History, Economics, Psychology, Sociology)

Undergraduate Degree Courses

Our degree programs are affiliated with Bangalore University and are designed to provide in-depth knowledge and practical skills, preparing students for professional careers and further studies.

BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)

A comprehensive program covering various aspects of business management, marketing, finance, and human resources.

BCom (Bachelor of Commerce)

Focuses on accounting, finance, taxation, and business law, essential for careers in commerce and accounting.

BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications)

Provides a strong foundation in computer science and applications, preparing students for careers in the IT industry.
