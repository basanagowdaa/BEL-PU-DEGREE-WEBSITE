Pre-University (PU) Admissions

We offer a nurturing academic environment for students who have completed their SSLC/10th Grade. Admissions are purely merit-based, focused on overall development and performance.

Courses Offered

  • Science: PCMB, PCMC
  • Commerce: CEBA, HEBA
  • Arts: HESP, HEPS

Eligibility

  • Completed SSLC/10th Grade from a recognized board
  • Minimum 60% for Science, 50% for Commerce/Arts

Process

  1. Download and fill the PU application form
  2. Submit along with SSLC marks card and passport photo
  3. Attend counseling at college campus
  4. Confirm admission by paying first-term fees
